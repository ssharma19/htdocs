A postdoctoral Visiting Fellowship is available at Agriculture and AgriFood Canada (Eastern Cereal and Oilseed Research Centre, Ottawa, Ontario, Canada) to study reproductive transcriptomes of different species of the Brassicaceae and Poaceae. Details regarding the visiting fellowship can be found at http://www.nserc-crsng.gc.ca/Students-Etudiants/PD-NP/Laboratories-Laboratoires/index_eng.asp.

Applicants are expected to have a strong background in plant biology, molecular biology and bioinformatics, as well as experience analyzing next-generation sequencing data. Documents for applications should include a short cover letter, a CV, and names and contact information of at least 2 references. Questions and documents for applications should be addressed by e-mail to Dr. Laurian Robert (Laurian.Robert@agr.gc.ca) before September 10th 2014.
